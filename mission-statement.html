Our Mission Statement

We have always strived to be 'on top' of the party. There is nothing worse than being at a party with alot of people and not being able to be on your phone to look at people that aren't at the party. For that reason we have created lighted hats of all kinds that provide mobile WiFi service. As an added benefit, these hats are Bluetooth enabled, allowing the party host to control their lights. We'd like to think we are 'a head' of the industry.
