Our Mission Statement

We have always strived to be 'on top' of the party. In order to do that we create lighted hats of all kinds that provide mobile WiFi service. As an added benefit, these hats are Bluetooth enabled, allowing the party host to control their lights. We'd like to think we are 'a head' of the industry.
